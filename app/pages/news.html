AM Express has announced new service

10/26/2023 5:50 AM EST

AM Express has announced the launch of a new service for shipments between the U.S. and China The new service will enable AM Express customers to quickly and reliably deliver their shipments from China to the U.S. and vice versa.

With the new service, customers will be able to reduce the delivery time of their goods to a few days, which will significantly speed up the process of trade between the two countries. In addition, the company provides a wide range of services, including packaging and customs clearance.

The new service is already available to customers in New York and Los Angeles, and will soon be expanded to other US cities.
